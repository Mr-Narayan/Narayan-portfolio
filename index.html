About Narayan Prasad Pokhrel

Narayan Prasad Pokhrel is a dedicated civil servant serving as an Under Secretary in the Ministry of Education, Science and Technology, Nepal. Born in Gulmi Madane, he has devoted his career to advancing educational policies and fostering development in Nepal's education sector. His leadership and commitment have been instrumental in shaping policies that promote equitable access to quality education, particularly in underserved regions of Nepal.

With a deep-rooted passion for public service, Narayan has worked tirelessly to bridge educational disparities, drawing from his humble beginnings in a rural village to inform his empathetic and inclusive approach to policy-making. His vision is to empower future generations through education, ensuring that every child in Nepal has the opportunity to thrive.
